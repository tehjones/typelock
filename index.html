Why TypeLock

Two gaps in macOS. One tiny utility.

TypeLock handles the input-method choices macOS leaves up to you.

01

Choose your real default.

Pick any installed input method as your default. TypeLock restores it when macOS or another app tries to switch away.

02

Give each app its own rule.

Keep ABC in Visual Studio Code, your preferred input method in Messages, or leave an app unmanaged. TypeLock switches as your focus changes.

Why I made it

Built for one bilingual workflow.

I type in English and Chinese. I use WeChat Input for everyday typing because one tap of Shift switches between Chinese and English. In apps where I only type English, such as Visual Studio Code, I use ABC.

TypeLock keeps those choices in place. It works with any input method installed on your Mac, not only WeChat Input.

I built it for myself and made it open source in case it fits your workflow too.
